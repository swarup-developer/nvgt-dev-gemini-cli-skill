New in 1.0
Lowered down the wirebomb's damage a little.
There are now bloodbath teams you can have! Please note that the team limit is 5, so make sure you're sure who you want to add. Use /teamcreate to create, /teamjoin <password> to join a team, /teampass to update the password, and /t sends chats, /teampoints will check for team points used later to get coins, etc. Please note that adding someone to your team will not stop you from hitting them with weapons and bombs, it'll just merge kills and collect points.
Adds lockers you can place on the map! Get a team together, collect metal and create a locker! You can store 20 items of each in a locker, so store sparingly!
Finally installs keyhook for users that use jaws.

Fixes a very interesting bug with inventory syncing. No more waiting 10 seconds after you collected something or admins giving you items, for them to show up in the inventory menu/inventory cycle.

Added server-side controlled input boxes!

You will need to have a lot of health to survive this, or better hope you're quick enough to stop the person from starting the bomb...

Added poisonstars! Launch one and it will start going after the closest player. If it hits, that player will start walking slower and slower, and start losing health. If they are poisoned for long enough, they will be knocked out! While you are knocked out you will be unable to move, and you may or may not be able to hear what's around!
Added a sleep system! It's currently buggy though.

Added bombs and bomb launchers! These things are really cool! First, make sure you have some bombs to operate the launcher, then use the bomb launcher and watch as you place it. Then, asuming all goes well, a bomb will be dropped on any player within 100 feet of your location. Be careful however, if there are no players within a hundred feet of your location, the bomb will be dropped on you instead! Bombs come with very little warn time and a lot of damage, very good for catching players unawares! But that's not all. Remember I said if all goes well? Well, there's a chance of these randomly failing! If they fail you won't lose your launcher, but still, that's valuable time lost and your element of surprise has most likely vannished.
