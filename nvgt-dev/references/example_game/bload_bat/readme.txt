About the game.
In this game, your goal is simply to kill and not let yourself be killed.
You have a collection of weapons right off the bat which can be accessed using the number row. I'll leave you to figure out their different strengths and weaknesses, but weather you want to get in close and kill, go in at a more careful distance and pick your enemies off, or even just go in spraying bullets without a care in the world, you'll most likely be able to find something that works for you.
You can also find items that can do a number of things. Again, I'll leave you to figure out a lot of these for yourself.
Keys.
Up, down, left and right. Move in the respective direction.
Q / E. Turn left or right respectively.
F. Check facing direction.
Space. Jump.
Number row. Select weapons.
Control. Fire the selected weapon.
Tab or shift + Tab. Move forwards or backwards through your inventory respectively.
I. Opens the inventory menu, just another way you can access your inventory.
Z. Checks your zone. Zones are used to distinguish areas of a map.
T. Opens a menu where you can select a player to track.
W. Check where the currently tracked player is.
F1. Check server message of the day.
F2. Check server uptime.
F3. Test ping the server.
Enter. Use items.
Shift + Enter. Interact with things on the map. Currently used for disarming mines and for exiting the safe zone.
H. Checks your health.
/. Opens an input box where you can type a message to chat.
Shift + /. Check players online.
Left or right bracket. Moves backwards and forwards through the current buffers respectively.
Shift + left or right brackets. Moves to the top or bottom buffer respectively.
Comma and fullstop. Moves backwards or forwards through the current buffer.
Shift + comma or fullstop. Moves to the top or bottom of the current buffer.
C. Checks your current coordinates.
Escape. Exits things. Menues, input boxes, or the game.
The camera.
The camera allows you to see what's around you in the game. It can be useful for looking for edges of platforms, platforms above or below you, and locating a multitude of other things. Here's how it works.
To use the camera, you must be holding the G key. While the G key is held, the following keys are operational.
Up, down, left and right. Move the camera. It will play different sounds at it's location depending on what it finds.
Page up / down. Moves the camera up or down respectively.
T. Replays the sound at the camera's current tile.
D. Speaks what is at the camera's current tile. For example, if the camera is at 50, 50, 0, and you press D, it might say something like,
concrete at 50, 50, 0.
Meaning the camera detected the concrete tile at 50 50 0. This will tell you of multiple things that might be on the camera's tile if applicable.
Items.
Every few seconds, the server will spawn an item. Items make a little beeping sound. Just walk towards one to grab it.
Note that items will self destruct after 60 seconds if left uncollected, and if shot will break.
